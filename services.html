What Michel Offers

Practical culinary leadership for operations that expect more

Michel’s consulting services are built for hospitality environments that require more than surface-level improvement. The focus is on creating kitchens that operate with stronger standards, clearer workflows, disciplined execution, and long-term operational stability.

Whether supporting a new opening, refining an existing concept, or restructuring a kitchen for better performance, each engagement is shaped around the same goal: to align culinary identity with systems, training, and service standards that hold up in real operation.

Culinary direction, operational clarity, and systems designed to perform under real service pressure.

Selected Consulting Services

Menu Engineering & Recipe Standardization
