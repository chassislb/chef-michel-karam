About Michel Karam

Michel Karam is an Executive Chef and culinary consultant with over 25 years of experience across luxury hotels, resorts, diplomatic institutions, and flagship hospitality operations in the Middle East and beyond.

His work is defined by disciplined kitchen leadership, concept development, and the ability to build high-performance culinary environments where quality, consistency, and operational excellence go hand in hand.
