The Chef

A culinary identity shaped by standards, taste, and composure

Michel Karam is an Executive Chef whose work is defined by refinement, structure, and an instinctive understanding of premium hospitality. His approach goes beyond creating beautiful plates. It is about building an experience that feels considered from the first detail to the last.

Over the course of his career, he has worked in demanding culinary environments where consistency, discipline, and guest expectation are non-negotiable. That background shaped a style that is elegant without being excessive, creative without losing control, and ambitious without compromising standards.

Today, Michel brings that same mindset into both executive chef roles and culinary consulting, helping concepts elevate not only what is served, but how the entire operation feels behind the scenes.

Refined hospitality through disciplined execution

Culinary Philosophy

Creativity is strongest when it is supported by discipline

Michel’s philosophy is rooted in balance. Great cuisine should feel expressive, memorable, and precise, but the strength behind it comes from structure, leadership, and respect for the craft.

In his kitchens, creativity is never separated from standards. Every menu, every service, and every guest touchpoint should reflect clarity of vision as well as consistency of execution.

This is what defines his work: a belief that hospitality becomes truly elevated when the visible beauty of the experience is matched by calm, intelligent, well-led performance behind it.
